Getting Started

Payments & Donations

We currently support Credit/Debit Cards, Mobile Money (MTN, Vodafone, AirtelTigo), and Bank Transfer. All transactions are encrypted and processed securely.

Managing Campaigns

Yes. Go to My Profile → My Campaigns and click Edit next to the campaign you want to update. You can change the title, description, media, and deadline. The target amount and raised total cannot be reduced.
Go to My Profile → Withdraw Funds. Enter the amount, select your preferred payment method, and provide your account details. Withdrawal requests are reviewed within 2–3 business days before funds are released.
When a campaign's deadline passes, it is marked as Ended. All funds raised up to that point remain available for withdrawal. Campaigns can still receive donations even after the deadline in some cases — contact support for assistance.

Safety & Trust

On any campaign page, click the "Report" button and describe your concern. Our moderation team reviews all reports within 24 hours. Campaigns found to be fraudulent are removed immediately.
FundRise takes your privacy seriously. We do not sell your data to third parties. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. You can delete your account at any time by contacting support.
